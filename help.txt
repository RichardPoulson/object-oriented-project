How to Play Checkers:The object of the game is to capture all of your opponent’s checkers or position your pieces so that your opponent has no available moves. Movement:Basic movement is to move a checker one space diagonally forward.Move a checker piece diagonally forward and to the left with moveType ‘moveLeft’.Move a checker piece diagonally forward and to the right with moveType ‘moveRight’.Jumping:If one of your opponent’s checkers is on a forward diagonal next to one of your checkers, and the next space beyond the opponent’s checker is empty, then your checker can jump the opponent’s checker and land in the space beyond.Your opponent’s checker is captured and removed from the board. Jump a checker piece diagonally forward and to the left with moveType ‘jumpLeft’.Jump a checker piece diagonally forward and to the right with moveType ‘jumpRight’.Game Termination:The game ends when all of one of the player’s pieces have been captured.This player who ran out of pieces is the loser of the game.The competing player is the winner of the game.Sources:https://www.itsyourturn.com/t_helptopic2030.html
